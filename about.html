About RateFlix

Where cinema meets conversation.

RateFlix Movies is a movie discovery, rating and review platform built for movie lovers across languages, cultures and genres. Browse cinema, watch trailers, rate films, save favourites and revisit stories worth remembering.

🎞 Discover Movies

Browse Marathi, Bollywood, Tamil, Telugu, Hollywood and animated cinema.

⭐ Rate & Review

Rate movies based on your experience and leave audience reviews.

❤️ Wishlist

Save films and return whenever you’re ready to watch.

▶ Official Trailers

Watch official trailers directly from the platform.

Our Vision

Great cinema always starts a conversation. RateFlix exists to continue that conversation after the credits roll — through reviews, ratings, trailers and rediscovery.
